Avocado Bagel

Description

An amazing avocado bagel.

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Avocado
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Chili flakes
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Toast the bagel
  2. Mash an avocado
  3. Add salt and sesame oil to avocado mash, then mix
  4. Add avocado mash onto bagel
  5. Season with black pepper and chili flakes
